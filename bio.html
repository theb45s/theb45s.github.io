Who Are The B45's?

The B45's are a band of adolescents, from the Fort Wayne, Indiana area, made up of Colin Taylor, Ben Tarr, Kellen Baker, and Sam Clay. They wish to incorporate their 60's Rock n' roll influences with a modern and youthful energy and create original music that resonates and introduces a progressive nostalgia.

Their vocal harmonies, screeching guitar riffs, and steady canon drums make for a high energy and electrified set of music, easily enjoyed by all ages.

This band, while still only about a year and a half old, has achieved a lot playing at venues such as The Brass Rail, Foellinger Theatre, and Fort Wayne's Embassy Theatre. Building up a large catalog of original music, The B45's wish to keep expanding their musical possibilities and continue their success.
